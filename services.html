Our approach

How an engagement actually unfolds.

Each engagement is shaped to your firm — but the underlying rhythm is consistent. Listening first. Clarity that holds up. Capability that lasts.

Listen and understand

Conversations with partners, managers, and (where helpful) clients. An honest read of where the firm actually stands.

Sharpen the focus

Clarity on positioning, target clients, pricing, and commercial priorities — agreed by the people who will execute it.

Build the capability

Coaching, frameworks, and the rituals that make growth feel doable inside busy client-service teams.

Run with you

We sit alongside your team while the rhythm bedds in — real conversations, real reviews, quick course corrections.

Embed and hand over

The work belongs to your firm. We're easy to remove when the rhythm is running without us.

Stay close

Most firms keep us on a light advisory retainer afterwards — useful for tricky pursuits, partner reviews, and quiet counsel.
